About the Job

We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced Senior GenAI Research Engineer to join our core AI reasoning team at Google DeepMind. You will play a critical role in developing and deploying scalable Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) solutions that power our next-generation intelligent agents.

Responsibilities

  • Design, build, and optimize large-scale RAG pipelines.
  • Fine-tune and deploy LLMs for reasoning and reasoning-augmented tasks.
  • Collaborate across cross-functional teams to integrate AI models into production.
  • Research and propose state-of-the-art NLP implementations.

Requirements

  • 5+ years of software engineering experience in Python.
  • Deep understanding of LLM architectures and prompting paradigms.
  • Hands-on experience with vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, Milvus).
  • Experience scaling machine learning workloads using PyTorch and Databricks.
